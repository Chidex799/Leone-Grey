About the company

Leone and Grey is a Men’s Wear brand that specializes in bespoke tailoring and ready to wear (RTW). With a commitment to individuality and sophistication, Leone and Grey offers discerning gentlemen the opportunity to express their unique style through tailor-made garments. Each piece is meticulously handcrafted, ensuring a perfect fit and attention to detail that's second to none. We sell quality fabrics, which most are sourced from Turkey alongside men’s accessories to match and sold at affordable market prices. From Fabric to Fit, Leone and Grey embodies the essence of sartorial excellence, making every wearer feel like a true connoisseur of fashion. Motto: Style & Confidence.
